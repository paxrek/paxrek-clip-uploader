Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Paxrek Clip Uploader is a private creator tool used by Paxrek to upload edited gaming clips to Paxrek-owned TikTok accounts. This policy explains what information the tool uses and how it is handled.

Information Used

The tool may use TikTok account authorization tokens, basic TikTok account identity information returned during authorization, selected video files, captions, hashtags, and local upload records.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to authenticate Paxrek-owned TikTok accounts and upload selected video files to TikTok for review, posting, or scheduling.

Storage

Authorization tokens and upload records are stored locally on the creator's computer. The tool does not operate a public user database.

Sharing

Information is not sold or shared with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties. Video files and account authorization data are sent to TikTok only as needed to use TikTok's official posting features.

Data Deletion

Local tokens and upload records can be deleted from the creator's computer at any time. TikTok account access can also be revoked from TikTok account settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Paxrek creator through the official Paxrek social channels.
